# S2-03 Mode C v4 Quality Exemplar

Use this exemplar for method only, not for story style. It teaches causality, one attention center, visible performance, continuity, dialogue/lip-sync boundaries, and low-reroll structure; it must not donate its genre, soft lighting, urban/comedy rhythm, clothing, location, character relationship, or specific prop event to another project.

## Why this example is stable

- One attention center: 沈星洲停步。
- One supporting reaction: 陆序回望。
- One camera motion: rightward lateral follow, then settle; no competing push/zoom/pan.
- Three continuous beats cover 0.0–5.8 seconds without overlap or gaps.
- Wide-shot performance uses steps, weight, shoulder direction, mouth state, and spatial pressure; it does not request pupil, eyelid, or lip-line detail.
- Lighting stays at one motivated 3200K source family; shot size does not change color temperature.
- Background guests are treated as one low-amplitude layer instead of receiving individual micro-actions.
- QA purpose, action counts, and performance causality live in `qa_metadata`; negative words live in `negative_prompt`.

## Performance design

Start state: 主角穿过人群，对手仍在社交。

Trigger: 主角听见对手方向的交谈。

Primary response: 步伐停止，重心收回，肩线和视线定向。

Supporting response: 对手只转头、闭口、停住交谈，不迎上去。

Residual end state: 两人隔着人群互相看见，但没有立即靠近，关系压力留在画面中。

Physical logic: 没有接触或外力；停止行进、回望和维持距离都是角色自主反应，提示词不把心理变化伪装成机械制服。

Motion boundary: 主角的行进被结束，但肩线与视线仍可定向；对手只被允许回望，不追加靠近。

Hold strategy: 落幅停顿只保留闭口和相互视线，不用密集微动作填满静止。

Viewer empathy anchor: 观众不是因为提示词写“感染力强”而共情，而是因为看见角色听到关键信息后脚步突然收住、没有靠近、只隔着人群看向对方。这个可见瞬间让观众直接读到“他被这句话刺中，但还在克制”。

Readable image moment: 本镜只保留一个主画面证据——步伐停止后重心收回、肩线定住、视线穿过人群落到对手身上。它同时服务表演、构图和跨镜承接，不额外增加手部、嘴角、泪光等不可见或抢戏细节。

## Low-reroll principle

If a desired performance needs a second emotional turn, another camera direction, or another physical exchange, split it into the next subshot instead of expanding this prompt.

For a 10–15 second continuous dialogue, a single causal attention handoff is not a second dramatic focus. Keep one overall objective and choose only one execution strategy: fixed two-shot plus one rack focus, one unidirectional reframe, or actor blocking with a fixed camera. See `format_example.txt` shot `S3-01-01`. Repeated A→B→A focus changes or physical movement combined with rack focus must be split.

For spoken dialogue, see `S3-01-01`: every event locks the reference, type, speaker, and verbatim text, then binds its time range to a visible facial state, body state, delivery, and lip-sync rule. Each line appears exactly once in the performance timeline and is not repeated in the sound section.

For internal monologue, see `S4-01-01`: the OS owner remains explicit, the visible owner receives facial and body performance during the same time range, and `lip_sync=false` is reflected by a closed mouth. If the owner is offscreen or nonphysical, facial/body fields must state `N/A` with the reason instead of inventing a face.
