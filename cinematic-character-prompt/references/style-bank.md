# Cinematic Character Style Bank

Use this file only when the prompt needs richer vocabulary or sharper aesthetic selection.

## Eastern Aesthetic Lanes

- **Chinese court / palace**: layered hanfu or ruqun, crossed collar, wide sleeves, cloud-shoulder, silk gauze outer robe, woven brocade, gold-thread embroidery, jade pendant, buyao hairpin, pearl tassels, carved lacquer, screen shadows, candlelit palace corridor.
- **Wuxia / jianghu**: narrow-sleeved robe, leather belt, cloth boots, sword tassel, wind-worn fabric, travel dust, bamboo forest, rain on stone steps, restrained heroism, practical elegance.
- **Xianxia / mythic**: translucent silk layers, celestial embroidery, moon-white or ink-blue palette, jade crown, flowing sleeves, mist, backlit clouds, ritual symmetry, ethereal but physically coherent fabric.
- **Japanese period / modern**: kimono structure, obi, haori, hakama, subtle textile patterns, lacquer hair ornament, shoji light, restrained asymmetry, seasonal color cues.
- **Korean historical / modern**: hanbok silhouette, jeogori, chima, norigae ornament, soft volume, court hair, clean facial styling, pale morning light, elegant restraint.

## Western Aesthetic Lanes

- **Renaissance / Baroque**: structured bodice, velvet, pearls, lace collar, gold filigree, painterly chiaroscuro, candlelight, rich jewel tones, composed dignity.
- **Gothic / medieval fantasy**: high collar, embroidered cloak, leather or chain details, silver clasps, cathedral shadows, cold rim light, stone arches, solemn grandeur.
- **Victorian / Edwardian**: tailored coat, corseted silhouette, lace gloves, brooch, layered skirts, pocket watch, gaslight haze, muted earth and jewel tones.
- **Hollywood noir**: trench coat, satin dress or sharp suit, wet pavement, Venetian blind shadows, cigarette-smoke atmosphere without glamorizing smoking, low-key contrast.
- **Modern editorial cinema**: tailored minimal clothing, textured coat, subtle jewelry, practical makeup, urban reflections, soft key light, restrained color grade.

## Hybrid Guidance

- Choose one dominant tradition and one accent tradition.
- Combine silhouette from the dominant tradition with material, ornament, or color logic from the accent tradition.
- Keep hair, jewelry, and props culturally coherent unless the user asks for deliberate fantasy fusion.
- Example: "Ming-inspired crossed-collar robe with Western military tailoring in the shoulders, matte black silk, oxidized silver clasps, restrained Baroque embroidery."

## Costume Detail Checklist

Include 4-7 of these when relevant:

- Silhouette: robe, gown, armor, suit, cloak, jacket, skirt volume, sleeve shape.
- Layering: inner garment, outer robe, cape, scarf, veil, collar.
- Fabric: silk, gauze, satin, velvet, wool, linen, leather, brocade, organza.
- Craft: embroidery, jacquard, beadwork, appliqué, quilting, carved metal, lacquer.
- Fastening: sash, belt, clasp, buttons, lacing, pins, frog closures.
- Ornament: hairpin, crown, earrings, necklace, pendant, rings, brooch, tassel.
- Wear state: pristine ceremonial, rain-damp, battle-worn, travel-stained, faded heirloom.
- Story clue: rank insignia, family crest, hidden weapon, mourning color, repaired seam.

## Natural Expression Bank

- Quiet resolve: calm eyes, relaxed mouth, barely tightened jaw.
- Suppressed grief: eyes moist but focused, lips pressed softly, breath shallow.
- Courtly restraint: faint smile, lowered chin, gaze slightly aside.
- Suspicion: one brow subtly lifted, eyes tracking off-frame, shoulders still.
- Tenderness: softened gaze, half smile, open posture, warm catchlight.
- Exhaustion: eyelids heavy, face pale, posture held together by will.
- Awe: parted lips, widened eyes, chin lifted into the light.

## Cinematic Light And Camera

- Soft window key light, cool shadows, warm skin highlights.
- Candlelit chiaroscuro, amber highlights, deep velvet shadows.
- Moonlit rim light, blue-silver edge, mist diffusion.
- Overcast daylight, low contrast, porcelain skin texture.
- Sunset backlight, golden haze, silhouette separation.
- Neon practicals, wet reflections, teal-magenta accents.
- Lens options: 35mm environmental portrait, 50mm natural perspective, 85mm intimate portrait, anamorphic widescreen, shallow depth of field.
- Composition: rule of thirds, centered ritual symmetry, negative space, foreground veil/silk/bamboo, layered depth, over-the-shoulder tension.

## Negative Prompt Seeds

Use selectively:

bad anatomy, extra fingers, fused fingers, malformed hands, distorted face, cross-eyed, plastic skin, waxy skin, over-smoothed texture, stiff expression, exaggerated smile, overexposed highlights, muddy shadows, flat lighting, cheap costume, random ornaments, incorrect clothing closure, cluttered background, unreadable silhouette, text, watermark, logo
